About Me

Hello! My name is Victor Kimaru and I am a passionate web developer from Nairobi, Kenya. I have been working in the field of web development for over 3 years now.

I started my journey in technology when I was in high school. I was fascinated by how websites worked and decided to learn how to build them myself. Since then, I have never looked back.

My Background

I was born and raised in Kenya. I completed my primary education at Nairobi Primary School and went on to study at Kenya High School. Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the University of Nairobi.

My Interests

Apart from coding, I enjoy:

  • Reading technology blogs and articles
  • Playing football with friends
  • Listening to music
  • Traveling and exploring new places
  • Learning new programming languages

My Goals

My goal is to become a full-stack developer and work on projects that make a positive impact on people's lives. I believe technology can solve many problems in our society.
